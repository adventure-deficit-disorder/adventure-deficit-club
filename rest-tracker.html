Understanding the KPIs

Personal Power = how much control you have right now. It blends your Energy with how much uncertainty you’re exposed to.

Weighted Stress combines how hard stress hits (Intensity) and how long it lasts (Duration).

Complexity Index (CX) reflects coordination cost as moving parts grow.

Surface Uncertainty (SAU) captures exposure to failure points:

SAU = 0.6 × Weighted Stress + 0.4 × CX

Personal Power merges Energy and control into one score:

Personal Power = average(Energy, 100 − SAU)

Efficacy Lift shows momentum week‑over‑week:

Efficacy Lift (%) = ((Current Personal Power − Baseline Personal Power) ÷ Baseline) × 100

First time using the report? Your last‑week inputs become the baseline. Efficacy Lift appears after your next entry.
